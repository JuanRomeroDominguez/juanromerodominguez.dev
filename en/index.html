About me

SAP professional with more than 10 years of experience in critical projects on R/3 and S/4HANA environments, specialized in SAP IS-U processes and focused on designing, building, and executing complex technical solutions that deliver tangible, sustainable, and maintainable results.

I combine full-stack frontend and backend experience with solid knowledge of infrastructure, networking, performance, and system operations. This allows me to approach projects end to end: functional and technical design, development, integration, deployment, optimization, and support.

I also design and maintain my own production systems based on high-performance hardware such as DELL PowerEdge R630 and MikroTik CCR2116-12G-4S+, controlling each technological layer: code, servers, network, security, performance, monitoring, and availability.

As an independent developer I also create and publish Android applications for international marketplaces such as Google Play, Huawei AppGallery, Samsung Galaxy Store, and other mobile ecosystems. I work with a worldwide vision: app compatibility, store listings, icons, screenshots, promotional images, texts, translations, documentation, privacy policies, legal terms, consent, analytics, stability, and publishing review.

I develop my own projects in Android, web, PHP, .NET, SAP, automation, and integrations. The main images, texts, designs, code, and materials of my projects are created and maintained by me, except for third-party libraries, SDKs, APIs, services, or components when applicable.

I have experience in technical leadership, team management and coordination, bringing strategic vision, technical rigor, and a constant commitment to quality, scalability, and innovation.
