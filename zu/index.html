Mayelana nami

Uchwepheshe we-SAP onolwazi olungaphezu kweminyaka eyi-10 kumaphrojekthi abalulekile endaweni ye-R/3 kanye ne-S/4HANA, ekhethekile ezinqubweni ze-SAP IS-U futhi egxile ekuklameni, ekwakheni, nasekusebenziseni izixazululo zezobuchwepheshe eziyinkimbinkimbi eziletha imiphumela ebonakalayo, esimeme, nelondolozekayo.

Ngihlanganisa isipiliyoni esigcwele isitaki esingaphambili kanye ne-backend ngolwazi oluqinile lwengqalasizinda, amanethiwekhi, ukusebenza, kanye nokusebenza kwesistimu. Lokhu kungivumela ukuthi ngibhekane namaphrojekthi ekupheleni kuye ekupheleni: kusukela ekwakhiweni okusebenzayo nokwezobuchwepheshe kuye ekuthuthukisweni, ukuhlanganiswa, ukuthunyelwa, ukuthuthukiswa, nokusekelwa okuqhubekayo.

Ngiphinde ngiklame futhi ngigcine amasistimu ami okukhiqiza asekelwe ku-hardware esebenza kakhulu, njenge-DELL PowerEdge R630 ne-MikroTik CCR2116-12G-4S+, elawula zonke izendlalelo zobuchwepheshe: ikhodi, amaseva, inethiwekhi, ukuphepha, ukusebenza, ukuqapha, nokutholakala. Kumaphrojekthi ewebhu ngibheka imiphumela elinganisekayo, ehlanganisa ukulungiselelwa okuthuthukile kanye nesilinganiso esiphezulu samaphuzu e-PageSpeed ​​Insights uma umongo wobuchwepheshe ukuvumela.

Njengonjiniyela ozimele, ngiphinde ngidale futhi ngishicilele izinhlelo zokusebenza ze-Android zezimakethe zamazwe ngamazwe njenge-Google Play, i-Huawei AppGallery, i-Samsung Galaxy Store, namanye ama-ecosystems eselula. Ngisebenza ngombono womhlaba wonke: Ngilungiselela izinhlelo zokusebenza zabasebenzisi abavela emazweni ahlukene, abakhiqizi, namadivayisi, ukunakekela ukusebenzisana, ukulondoloza uhlu, izithonjana, izithombe-skrini, izithombe zokuphromotha, imibhalo, ukuhumusha, amadokhumenti, izinqubomgomo zobumfihlo, imigomo yezomthetho, imvume, izibalo, ukuzinza, nokubuyekezwa kokushicilelwe.

Ngithuthukisa amaphrojekthi wami ku-Android, iwebhu, i-PHP, i-.NET, i-SAP, ama-automation, kanye nokuhlanganisa. Izithombe eziyinhloko, imibhalo, imiklamo, ikhodi, nezinto zokusebenza kumaphrojekthi ami zidalwe futhi zinakekelwa yimi, ngaphandle kwamalabhulali ezinkampani zangaphandle, ama-SDK, ama-API, amasevisi, noma izingxenye ezisetshenziswa lapho kufanele khona.

Nginolwazi ebuholini bezobuchwepheshe, ukuphatha, nokuxhumana kweqembu, okuletha umbono wamasu, ukuqina kobuchwepheshe, nokuzibophezela okuqhubekayo kukhwalithi, ukuqina, kanye nokusungula izinto ezintsha. Ngigxila ezinseleleni ezidinga amakhono obuchwepheshe athuthukile kanye nombono womhlaba wonke okwazi ukuxhumanisa ibhizinisi, isofthiwe, ingqalasizinda, kanye nokusebenza kwangempela.
